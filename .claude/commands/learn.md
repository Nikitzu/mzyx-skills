Invoke the `mzyx-skills:learn` skill.

Use when diving into an unfamiliar domain or compiling multi-source research into a structured output. Two modes:

- **Deep Research** — produces a publish-ready draft (Phases 1–6)
- **Quick Reference** — builds a working mental model fast, notes only (Phase 2 exit)

Pairs with `/read` for fetching sources and `/write` for final prose polish. For a single URL with no research synthesis, use `/read` directly.
